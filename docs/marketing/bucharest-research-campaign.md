# Bucharest research recruitment campaign

Status: owner-ready draft. Do not publish, contact venues, collect data, buy media, or create accounts until the owner approves the audience and a reviewed research privacy notice, inbox, retention period, and safety contact exist.

## Campaign job

Recruit research participants and credible founding hosts for the Aviatorilor-Herastrau / Aurel Vlaicu-Floreasca hypothesis. This is not a product launch and must not imply active events, member volume, venue partnerships, safety certification, or guaranteed access.

Primary call to action: **Apply for a 30-minute research conversation.**

Secondary host call to action: **Discuss hosting four small pilot sessions.**

This is a research conversation, not an active dating service or event registration. We will show two concrete event ideas, listen to what would make you join or refuse, and ask how intentions, privacy, price, level, and safety should work.

## Research form fields

Collect the minimum needed to screen and schedule:

- age band: 25-29 / 30-34 / 35-39 / outside current study;
- relationship status confirmation: currently single / prefer not to answer;
- broad home or work access area, not an address;
- realistic maximum one-way travel time;
- activity in the last 60 days and frequency;
- run/run-walk, padel, or bouldering reserve interest;
- Romanian / English conversation preference;
- two real availability windows;
- stated price tolerance for padel;
- optional accessibility accommodation request in free text, handled as sensitive access-limited data;
- email or phone for scheduling, collected separately from research responses;
- permission to contact for this study, separate optional permission for future product research.

Show the controller identity, research purpose, lawful basis selected with counsel, recipients, retention, rights, withdrawal route, and complaint route before submission. Do not pre-check permission or reuse research contact details for marketing.

## Measurement plan

### Funnel

1. Qualified landing visit: correct campaign and corridor context seen.
2. Form start.
3. Eligible completed application.
4. Scheduled interview.
5. Completed interview.
6. Behavior-backed event choice at a real time and price.
7. Permission for a later pilot invitation.

Report counts and conversion by source, language, sport, and broad corridor fit. Suppress small demographic slices and never optimize ads using sensitive dating, orientation, health, or accessibility data.

### Campaign tags

Use a stable convention such as:

`utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=bucharest_research_01&utm_content=carousel_hypothesis_en`

Allowed sources initially: founder referral, host referral, Instagram organic, LinkedIn organic, community referral. Paid source tags wait for explicit budget and platform approval.

### Decision signals

- Primary: completed interviews with a concrete next-30-day event choice.
- Supply: host candidates who meet the rubric and provide four actual dates.
- Friction: price, travel, intent mismatch, level anxiety, safety, privacy, or schedule refusal.
- Integrity: ineligible rate, duplicate rate, consent withdrawal, and source concentration.

Likes, impressions, poll votes, and follower growth are creative diagnostics only. They are not product-market evidence.

## Publication checklist

- Owner approves the corridor, age hypothesis, sports, and copy.
- Research privacy notice and retention are reviewed.
- Scheduling inbox, access control, deletion route, and incident owner exist.
- Final brand/name and handle are checked before account creation.
- Every venue/community reference is factual and does not imply partnership.
- Actual padel price and equipment terms are rechecked on publication day.
- Link, consent separation, language, accessibility, analytics, and deletion are tested.
- A human is assigned to respond; no unattended direct-message recruitment.
