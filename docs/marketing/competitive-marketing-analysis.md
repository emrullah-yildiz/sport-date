## 2. AtClub deep-dive (the app the owner named)

**Confidently identified.** AtClub — *"AtClub – Sports Partners"* / *"AtClub – Parteneri
sportivi"* (`com.atclub.app`, developer **ACCESS WORLD**) — is a proximity-based
sports-partner matching app (50+ sports, discovery + chat), **localised for Romania** and
also live in Brazil. Tagline **"Sport and chat."**

- **Strengths:** direct, simple value prop (find someone to play with nearby); already
  operating in **Romania** (our beachhead market); broad sport coverage.
- **Weaknesses / our opening:** an **aggressive pay-to-play paywall** — core social
  functions (seeing messages, distance filters, likes, age filter) are locked behind a
  subscription reported around **80 BRL/week (~€60/mo-equivalent)**. That's a swipe-app
  monetization model bolted onto sports. It optimises for extraction, not for people
  actually meeting.
- **Net:** AtClub is the closest direct competitor in our market, and its weakness is
  exactly our thesis: it monetizes *attention/access*, we monetize *outcomes*. Meeting on
  KeepItUp is free; safety is free.

## 3. Recommendations for KeepItUp (prioritized)

**⭐ #1 — highest leverage: short-form video around real meetups + seed local run/sport clubs.**
The Lunge proof (30→1,000/week on viral TikTok) is the exact motion our agent-run organic
engine is built for. Double down: capture/stage real meetup moments, partner with and show
up at existing Bucharest run clubs / padel groups, and let the content carry it. This is our
cheapest, most defensible growth — and it's already switched on.

2. **Position explicitly against pay-to-message (vs AtClub).** Make "meeting is always free,
   safety is always free" a loud, repeated message. AtClub locks messaging behind ~€60/mo;
   we don't. This is a direct, honest wedge in the *same* market.
3. **Solve liquidity with a host/ambassador program (Bumble Honey model).** Recruit local
   sport organisers (run-club leaders, padel bookers) as hosts/ambassadors — the two-sided
   cold-start answer. Bumble scaled on exactly this.
4. **Borrow Timeleft's "curated group" mechanic for cold start.** Don't wait for hosts —
   *we* organise the first small events at set times in one neighbourhood, so a new member
   always finds something to join. Curate liquidity into existence, then let hosts take over.
5. **Venue/club partnerships** (padel courts, climbing gyms, running stores) — co-marketed
   events create instant local density and credibility.
6. **Own Bucharest first, against AtClub's gap** — ethical pricing, multi-intent (dating +
   friendship + community, not just "sports partner"), trust-first. Be the *humane* AtClub.
7. **SEO/content** on "alternatives to dating apps," "meet people through sport," "run club
   dating" — capture the exact intent the Lunge phenomenon shows is surging.
8. **Bring-a-friend / shareable invites** (already built) — cheapest viral loop; lean on it.

**What to avoid:** AtClub/Sports Mates-style paywalls on core social functions; overpricing
(£24.99 is a cautionary tale); and chasing broad national launch before one city has real
density.
