**Status:** draft

game. The exact venue is revealed only after the host accepts your request to join.
**Pillar:** Feature-led (new) · **Hypothesis:** H-FEATURE-privacy (a serious, trust-building
explainer about the privacy design wins saves/shares as well as our science-fact posts do)

**Overlay text:** "WE NEVER SHOW YOUR EXACT LOCATION. NOT EVEN TO SOMEONE YOU HAVEN'T MET YET."

The exact place only shows up once the host says yes and lets you into the game. Until then, nobody can pin down exactly where you are, not even someone you're already talking to.

We built it this way on purpose. You should be able to look around before anyone can find you. KeepItUp is open worldwide — early access, link in bio."

## P2 — IG + TikTok image · Feature: not a swiping app
**Status:** draft
**Feature (audit-verified):** there is no open browsing of people on KeepItUp. You can only view
someone's profile if you're the host or the requester on a game together, or you've both been
in an accepted game together.
**Pillar:** Feature-led (new) · **Hypothesis:** H-FEATURE-notswiping (the core differentiator —
"we match you over a game, not a grid of faces" — out-performs generic dating-app jokes on
follows, since it states our actual mechanism instead of just poking fun)
**Photo:** `/brand/social/photos/pexels-33920366-flag-football.jpg` (golden hour, high energy, bold)
**Treatment:** bold manifesto-style overlay, centered, dark scrim behind the text.

**Overlay text:** "NO GRID OF FACES. NO ENDLESS SCROLLING. JUST A GAME."
**Caption:** "KeepItUp doesn't have a big list of people to scroll through. There's no grid of faces to swipe left or right on.

Here's what you actually see: games near you. You ask to join one, or someone asks to join yours. Only then — once you're both in the same game — can you see each other's profile.

We match you over something you're both doing, not a photo you both liked. KeepItUp is open worldwide — early access, link in bio."
**On-screen text:** overlay line above.
**Hashtags:** #NotASwipingApp #RealGamesRealPeople #MeetThroughMovement #KeepItUp #NoGridOfFaces
