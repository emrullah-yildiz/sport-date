creative territory is collective suspense: extra time, penalties, tactical group
chats, and the difference between watching movement and joining it.

Primary source: [FIFA match schedule](https://www.fifa.com/en/tournaments/mens/worldcup/canadamexicousa2026/articles/match-schedule-fixtures-results-teams-stadiums)

The 2026 Championships run from 29 June through 12 July. The useful KeepItUp

Primary source: [Wimbledon 2026 dates](https://www.wimbledon.com/pdf/AELTC_SUBMITTING_PHOTOGRAPHS_2026.pdf)

The Tour runs 4–26 July and began in Barcelona. Stage 2 on 5 July travels from
Tarragona to Barcelona; the opening days emphasize team timing, changing pace,
and helping one another through a hard route. This maps cleanly to KeepItUp’s
belief that how somebody moves with others says more than a polished bio.

Primary sources: [official 2026 route](https://www.letour.fr/en/overall-route),
[Barcelona Grand Départ](https://www.letour.fr/en/the-race/grands-departs/grand-depart-2026)
