## Kill/keep criteria — Friday 2026-07-17 review

Reviewed against the state of sends/replies as of Friday, with the
understanding that signups can lag a day or two behind a reply:

- **KEEP outreach as the lead bet** if: at least 3 of the 20 organizer DMs
  produced a reply (any tone) by Friday, OR at least 1 registered user is
  attributable to a DM/community-post click by Friday. Continue sending
  16-20 + Link's packs through the weekend.
- **KILL/rework outreach** if: 0 replies across all sent DMs by Friday AND
  0 attributable signups. In that case, Friday's action is to change the
  message (not the channel) — get one honest reply from the owner or a
  friendly contact about why the DM isn't landing, revise the draft, and
  restart with targets 6-20 using the fixed version. Do not silently keep
  sending an unproven message.
- **Sprint-level kill/keep** (Sunday 07-19): if fewer than 5 total new
  registered users by Sunday night, this is logged honestly in the
  07-19 growth memo as a miss, with the specific bottleneck named (no
  reach, reach-but-no-click, click-but-no-signup) using whatever signup/
  click data is actually available — never backfilled with a guess.
- **What would make us keep pushing outreach into next week regardless of
  the 5-user number:** any positive-reply rate above ~15% (3+/20), since
  that's evidence the channel works and just needs another cycle of
  targets, not a strategy change.
