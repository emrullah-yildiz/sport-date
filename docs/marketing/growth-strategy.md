## The 30-day sequence (from 2026-07-06)

**Week 1 (Jul 6–12) — attention.** Ride the World Cup window daily (photo-mode
until video unblocks). Ship the revision batch answering every owner comment
(branded-poster look, new photo variety). Keep both queues full; IG cap (10)
managed by refilling as posts publish.
**Week 2 (Jul 13–19) — first data + final.** Final-week World Cup posts peak
Jul 17–19. First measure→prune pass ~Jul 18: kill the weakest format/time, double
down on winners. Draft the host kit so it's ready the moment a city is picked.
**Week 3 (Jul 20–26) — convert attention.** Shift mix toward "why us" + feature
posts (drive link-in-bio clicks). Poster-share nudge experiment: does the
post-publish share prompt produce measurable visits?
**Week 4 (Jul 27–Aug 2) — density prep.** With owner's city call: curated first
events + ambassador outreach list. Without it: keep compounding follower growth
and re-raise the city decision in the standup.
