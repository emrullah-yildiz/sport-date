## Decision / action needed

Run (or approve running) the member/market surveys and the forum outreach drafted in
`docs/marketing/member-survey-and-forum-kit.md`. Per `references/escalation-policy.md`, **external
posting, publishing, account creation, and contacting real people are OWNER actions** — agents must
**not** post to forums, create accounts, run ads, DM strangers, or collect real member data. The drafts
are ready; execution is the owner's.

## What is ready (agent-produced drafts)

- **Two surveys** (`member-survey-and-forum-kit.md`, Part A): a short ~2-min screener-friendly survey and
  a longer opt-in discovery + willingness-to-pay survey — safe, non-leading questions about how people
  currently find workout partners, safety concerns, and willingness to pay.
- **Forum share kit** (Part B): neutral post-copy variants, a candidate-community list (subreddits/
  forums/clubs to *vet*, not an approved plan), and a rules-respecting posting plan — all marked DRAFTS
  for the owner.

## Recommendation

Run a **small, rules-compliant pilot**: stand up the survey behind a reviewed privacy notice, then post
Variant 1/2 in 2-3 survey-friendly communities **with mod permission**, measuring completion and
objection quality (not upvotes). Iterate copy on the first objections, then expand only if
well-received. Prefer asking mods first over cold posting.

## Two meaningful alternatives

- **A) Interviews-first (no public posting).** Recruit only via founder/host referrals per the existing
  Bucharest campaign; slower, smaller sample, but lowest reputational/rule risk.
- **B) Broad multi-community launch.** More reach, but high risk of rule violations, spam perception,
  and low-quality data; not recommended without a proven pilot first.

## Non-negotiable guardrails (regardless of choice)

- **Agents do not execute any external step** — no posting, no account creation, no DMs, no data
  collection, no ad spend.
- No data collection until a **reviewed privacy notice, controller identity, lawful basis, retention,
  deletion route, and a named human responder** exist (mirrors the campaign publication checklist).
- Honest framing only: it's *research*, not a live service; **no** implied members/traction, **no** claim
  on the positioning "Language to avoid" list.
- Sensitive questions (safety, relationship intent) stay optional; contact details collected separately
  with explicit permission; small demographic slices suppressed; WTP treated as directional only.
- A *branded* account should wait for the final name/handle (`CX-20260701-owner-decision-brand-name-and-logo`).

## Consequence of delay

No external market signal (how people find partners, safety concerns, WTP) is gathered, leaving several
product/pricing assumptions untested by real people. Nothing in-app is blocked.

## Exact action requested

3. Approve the pilot community list (after vetting each community's rules / getting mod permission) and
   post from an owner-run account — or delegate to a human, not an agent.
